EXAMPLES:

`Template String: 
<ul>
	{@each list as it,k}
		<li>${it.name} (index: ${k})</li>
	{@/each}
	{@each blah as it}
		<li>
			num: ${it.num} <br />
			{@if it.num==3}
				{@each it.inner as it2}
					${it2.time} <br />
				{@/each}
			{@/if}
		</li>
	{@/each}
</ul>
